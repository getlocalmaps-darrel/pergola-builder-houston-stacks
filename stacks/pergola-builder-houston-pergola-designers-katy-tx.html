A: Custom pergola pricing in Houston depends on size, materials, and design complexity. Basic pergolas start around $3,000-$5,000, while larger cedar or attached pergolas with lighting and fan hookups run $8,000-$15,000+. Call (832) 509-5457 for a free on-site estimate.

Q: Do you handle HOA approvals for patio covers?

A: Yes. Pergola Builder Houston handles all permits, drawings, and HOA documentation. We prepare site plans and elevation drawings that meet HOA submission requirements across Katy, Cinco Ranch, Sugar Land, and The Woodlands.

Q: How long does it take to build an outdoor kitchen?

A: Most outdoor kitchen projects take 2-5 days of construction once materials arrive. Design, permitting, and material lead times add 2-4 weeks before construction starts. Basic grill islands start around $5,000.

Q: Do your patio covers hold up in Houston storms?

A: Yes — our structures are engineered for Houston weather including high winds, heavy rain, and humidity. We use proper footings, hurricane-rated fasteners, and drainage design to handle Gulf Coast conditions.
